Essential Launcher

Essential Launcher is a very small launcher (< 30 KB APK) with all essential functionality.

Introduction

Save system resources with Essential Launcher. Contrary to all heavy-weight solution with 3D and many settings Essential Launcher offers the most important functionality in a very compact way. It only needs 300 KB disk space.

Main features:

  • Choose on selectable widget (long press "App drawer")
  • A dock with the most used apps.
  • Make apps sticky on the dock.
  • An app drawer with the ability to launch information about the app and uninstall them.
  • Accesibility ready.
  • Support for LTR and RTL texts.

Current limitations

  • Widgets are not automatically advanced.
